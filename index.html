Important FAQ

What is the purpose of the collective donation project?

The collective donation project aims to pool resources from multiple donors to support flood affected people, students injured in the movement and many other people affected by calamities such as providing education, healthcare, or disaster relief. By combining our efforts, we can maximize our impact and reach more people in need.

What percentage of my donation goes directly to the cause?

We are committed to transparency. Approximately 98% of your donation goes directly to funding our programs, while a small portion is allocated for administrative costs to ensure the sustainability of our organization.

Will I receive updates on how my donation is being used?

Yes! We send regular updates to our donors about the progress of our projects and how their contributions are making a difference. You can also follow us on social media for real-time updates.

How do you ensure accountability and transparency in your projects?

We maintain high standards of accountability by regularly auditing our financials and providing detailed reports on project outcomes. Our commitment to transparency means that donors can see exactly how their contributions are utilized.

How can I involve my friends or family in this project?

You can share information about the project through social media or our website. Additionally, consider starting a peer-to-peer fundraising campaign where you encourage others to donate on your behalf.
